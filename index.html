About

Born with a paint brush in hand, I have always been a creative and ingenuitive. When I was young, all I wanted to do was draw in school. I used to get in trouble for drawing on my tests and textbooks but I just couldn't help it. I loved drawing no matter where or when. When I would go to church, there would always be a little notebook in my hand and a pen in the other doodling the whole service. Eventually, I had notebooks piled everywhere and all of the notebooks I bought for school were filled with more drawings than they were notes. Creating art is where I find happiness and peace, even when it is perplexing. I knew from a young age that I had to go into an art career as I felt it was the only thing I was happy doing.
